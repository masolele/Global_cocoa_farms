This validation data comprises of 2,270 points distributed across Africa (760), Latin America (860), and Southeast Asia (650).

DESCRIPTION OF FIELD IDs IN THE VALIDATION SHAPEFILE:
Pred_Class: Predicted cocoa
Obs_Class: Observed cocoa
Obs_Name: Name of observed cocoa class
Conf_class: 1 stands for correct predictions, 2 for incorrect predictions

BELOW IS THE LIST OF IDs OF COCOA FARMING SYSTEMS AND THE CORRESPONDING LABELS/NAMES : 

[1: Fullsun cocoa,
 2: Forest shaded cocoa, 
 3: Tree-crop shaded cocoa, 
 4: Pasture cocoa, 
 5: Others
]
